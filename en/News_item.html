HUIT STARTUP 2025 – Student Startup Idea Competition at Ho Chi Minh City University of Industry 2025

May 16, 2025 • Mary Pham • News - Events

HUIT STARTUP 2025 – The startup idea competition for students at Ho Chi Minh City University of Industry officially returns for its 6th season with the theme:

"Innovation – Towards the Nation’s Era of Growth"

– Hey Gen Z and Alpha youth, have you ever had a "bold idea too daring to share"?

– Are you passionate about startups but still struggling to find your path?

– Do you want to turn creativity into action and aspirations into real value?

HUIT STARTUP 2025 is where you can:

  • – Unlock creative thinking
  • – Turn ideas into feasible business models

Competition Details

Schedule:

  • Registration deadline: May 18, 2025
  • Preliminary round: May 19 – June 10, 2025
  • Semi-finals: June 13 – July 26, 2025
  • Final round: August 1 – October 4, 2025

Diverse Fields of Participation:

  1. Industry and product manufacturing
  2. Healthcare, wellness, and beauty technology
  3. Agriculture, forestry, and fisheries
  4. Social impact business
  5. Education, culture, and tourism
  6. Food technology
  7. Financial services and banking

Scale in 2025:

More than 30 universities, colleges, and high schools in Ho Chi Minh City and neighboring provinces participate.

Attractive Prizes

Total prizes worth over 1 BILLION VND!

  • Over 114 million VND in cash, trophies, and certificates
  • Innovation and startup project incubation package by Ho Chi Minh City Department of Science and Technology worth 40 million VND/project
  • Startup support sponsorship package from enterprises worth 50 million VND/project
  • English scholarship package worth over 10 million VND per team member reaching the finals
  • 1:1 intensive mentoring package worth 30 million VND/project
  • Opportunities for fundraising, expanding networks, and reaching national and international platforms!
