ABOUT US

Formation History

The Vietnam Fundraising Exchange (VFX) was launched on July 15, 2022, during the pilot phase of the National Innovation Startup Support Center project, located on floors 7, 8, and 9 of the Ministry of Science and Technology building in Ho Chi Minh City.

VFX is an initiative by the founders of the Viet Startup Incubator (VSI), with the aim of building an innovative investment ecosystem alongside the startup ecosystem, creating diverse capital flows for early-stage startups. At the same time, it provides a diverse and attractive investment channel for pioneering investors.
